Your About Us page is one of the most important pages on your website. It’s also likely to be one of the most frequently visited. So you’d better make it good. Keep the following suggestions in mind: Much of today’s business is transacted over the web or over the phone without an in-person meeting. For that reason, your About Us page must do more than communicate what’s special about your company’s products or services; it also must inspire trust because without trust, you’ll never get the prospect to reach for his wallet.
